# Assessor Checklist

## Purpose

Use this checklist to make registry assurance evidence actionable during adoption review.

## Minimum acceptance criteria

- The evaluated target is explicitly identified by `target_id` and service URL.
- CTS and TSPP reports share the same `run_id` and `target_id`.
- The Combined Assurance Manifest validates without identity drift.
- The Public Assurance Summary discloses assurance level, status, freshness, limitations, and consumer relevance.
- AL3 and AL4 deployments include governance, audit, lifecycle, and redress evidence.

## Copy-ready language

The operator SHALL provide current machine-readable assurance evidence and SHALL disclose material limitations that affect relying-party or consumer decisions.
