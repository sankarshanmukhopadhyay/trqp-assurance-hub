# Governance policy (example)

**Document ID:** GOV-POL-001

**Scope:** This policy applies to the operation of the Trust Registry / Trust List publisher and all assurance artifacts emitted for the evaluated scope.

## 1. Roles and responsibilities
- **Operator** MUST designate an accountable owner for trust list publication and evidence bundle emission.
- **Security owner** MUST approve key management and rotation procedures.
- **Assurance owner** MUST maintain the evidence bundle inventory and retention schedule.

## 2. Control objectives
The operator MUST maintain documented controls covering:
- Trust list publication integrity
- Change management and approvals
- Incident response and post-incident remediation
- Key management and rotation
- Evidence generation and retention

## 3. Change management
- Material changes (schema, publication endpoints, signing keys, publication cadence) MUST be approved and recorded.
- Changes MUST be traceable to a ticket/issue identifier.

## 4. Incident response
- Incidents impacting integrity or availability MUST be logged.
- Remediation actions MUST be recorded and linked to the detected nonconformity.

## 5. Evidence and retention
- Evidence bundles MUST be retained for the declared retention period.
- Redaction rules MUST be documented when operational data is restricted.
